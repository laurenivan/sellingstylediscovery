Sales Role Assessment Quiz

For each statement, rate yourself from 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 4 (Strongly Agree).

1. I thrive on finding new business opportunities and actively seek out potential customers.
2. I enjoy the excitement of closing deals quickly and moving on to the next opportunity.
3. I focus on maintaining strong relationships with existing clients and ensuring they continue to see value in our products/services.
4. I am comfortable handling objections and rejection without letting it affect my confidence.
5. I prioritize keeping customers happy, solving their problems, and increasing their lifetime value.
6. I enjoy both bringing in new customers and working closely with existing ones to maintain and grow their accounts.
7. I am driven by hitting quotas and achieving short-term sales goals.
8. I take a consultative approach, working closely with customers to ensure they are using our solutions effectively.
9. I can switch between prospecting and nurturing relationships based on the needs of the business.
10. I prefer making quick sales rather than taking the time to develop deep, long-term client relationships.
11. I proactively check in with clients after a sale to ensure their continued success and satisfaction.
12. I measure my success by both the number of new deals I close and the growth of my existing accounts.
